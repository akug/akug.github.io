I am a PhD student at the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence ([BCAI](https://www.bosch-ai.com/)), Renningen and the Bio-inspired Circuits and Systems research group at the [University of Groningen](https://www.rug.nl/research/zernike/bio-inspired-circuits-and-systems/chicca-group/?lang=en).

My work concerns novel methods and architectures for spiking and artificial neural networks to process event-based vision data fast, efficiently and accurately.
